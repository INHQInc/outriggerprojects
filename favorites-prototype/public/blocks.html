PromoCardBlock — NOT FAVORITABLE

Block Type: PromoCardBlock (content cards used for editorial/marketing callouts)

Description: These are generic promotional or editorial cards used for marketing campaigns, brand messaging, content promotion, or feature announcements. Examples include "Plan Your Getaway", "Island Guides", "Special Messages", seasonal promotions, or corporate announcements.

Why Not Favoritable: These cards are not inventory items (properties, rooms, or offers). They are marketing/editorial content that changes frequently with campaigns and promotions. Adding hearts to these would clutter the UI and confuse users about what "favorites" means. Favorites should be reserved for bookable products and actionable items that directly support the booking funnel.

User Impact: When users search for their favorites, they expect to see resorts they want to book, room types they prefer, and special offers they're interested in — not general marketing messages. This maintains a clear, focused experience.
